Spotting the narrow gap, Dobby dives through with surprising agility, tucking and rolling just as the boulders crash together behind him. 
The impact sends a cloud of dust into the air, leaving Dobby slightly disheveled and coughing. 
He stands up, brushing off his now-dusty pillowcase outfit. 
"Dobby is okay! We will keep going!" he says to Grimble with a determined nod.

Together, they reach the vault’s final chamber, where the treasure chest gleams in the light. 
Grimble pulls it open with a triumphant grin, but their celebration is cut short by the sound of goblin guards approaching from the entrance.

“We’re out of time!” Grimble gasps.
