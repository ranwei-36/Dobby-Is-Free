The Weasleys, touched by Dobby’s efforts and entertained by his antics, invite him to stay at the Burrow. 

It becomes his second home, where he is loved and embraced as part of the family.

Dobby becomes an honorary Weasley, always adding humor and heart to their lives—whether by charming the kitchen pots to sing while cooking or knitting everyone garishly mismatched socks for Christmas.

“Dobby is a free elf,” he says with pride one evening as he helps Ginny decorate for Christmas, “and the happiest elf in the Burrow!”

The End
