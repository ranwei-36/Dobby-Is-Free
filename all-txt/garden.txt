The Weasleys’ garden was in turmoil. 
Garden gnomes, typically mischievous but manageable, had banded together, holding tiny protest signs made from twigs and leaves. 
They demanded fair treatment: softer landing spots when thrown, less spinning during evictions, and an upgrade from the old boots the Weasleys sometimes used to chase them off.

Dobby, visiting the Burrow, watched the chaos unfold as gnomes catapulted themselves at Ron and Ginny from bushes. 
“Dobby will help! Gnomes deserve fairness too... but not too much fairness.”
